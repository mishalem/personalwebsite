    This project serves as a strong demonstration of my Object-Oriented Programming skills and is divided into two parts. Firstly, I implemented the 'Canadian Experience,' which comprises 33 classes. As for the second part, I've introduced 'Harold's Music Machine,' which comprises 20 classes. I seamlessly integrated the Music Machine into the Canadian Experience to add background music to our movies. This feature plays music in the background.
